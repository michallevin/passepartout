Passepartout

Welcome to the journey around the world!

In Jule Verne's "Around the World in Eighty Days"
Mr. Fogg and his valet Jean Passepartout
attempted to travel around the world in 80 days.

While Fogg and Passepartout faced some great
adventures on their way across seas and continents,
you'll face questions regarding the countries
they both visited together.

In order to enter each country on your route,
you'll have to answer these questions correctly.

Like Passepartout, you'll reach new places and learn new things,
So let's be off now!
